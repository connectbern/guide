Community Guide

About Us

Our dream is simple: we want to bring people together in a warm, social, and open space — real connection, no business, no strange vibes 🌍💛

We are a free, non-profit community in Bern, open to everyone 👫✨. Our place is run collectively, with the goal of creating a welcoming home where people can meet, share, and feel safe — something that can be hard to find, especially while traveling 🧳💖.

Life here is flexible and not very formal 🌀. The space works because everyone contributes and helps one another 🤗💪.

Daily Life 🌞🌙

🏠 Living together feels better when…

🧹

We do a quick morning clean-up so the day starts fresh (sometime between 8 and 9 am, the earlier the better!) ✨

🌜

Around 9 pm we start preparing the space for a calm night

🛌

By 10 pm everything is ready for sleep

🤫

The official quiet hours begin at 11 pm (you can go to sleep earlier if you want)

💖

We all help keep things pleasant and welcoming

What matters most is staying active 💪, supportive 🤗, and enjoying the process 🌈 of moving the project forward!

Before You Come 📞💬

To avoid misunderstandings:

✅ Confirm with us first:

do not come without a short video call with one of us where we confirm your arrival.

🔄 Have a backup plan:

Even if we told you that you can come, please have an alternative in case something unexpected happens.

📝 Feedback round:

After the first two days, we do a feedback round to see how everything is working. In more than 90% of cases, everything flows smoothly 😊. However, if living together proves difficult, or if there is no real commitment to help, we cannot continue hosting people where the dynamics don't work well for the group 🚫. This helps keep the project healthy and enjoyable for everyone 💛.

🚪 Arrival coordination:

Be sure that before you come someone confirmed they will be there to open the door. If they are not there, please don't wait outside — come back later 🙂.

Final Note 🌻💖

✨ What matters most is enjoying the experience together while staying active in moving the project forward.

🌈 Everyone is welcome here. The community works thanks to the active participation of everyone.

🤗 The help is flexible and shared — you don't need to be an expert, just contribute where you feel comfortable, learn from others, and support the group 🌟.
